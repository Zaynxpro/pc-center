    1. Why do I write reviews?

      We want to hear your opinions. We want consumers to get the information they need to make smart buying choices. As a PC-Centre client, you can submit reviews for items listed on PC-Centre.com.pk. We encourage you to share your ideas, both favourable and unfavourable

    2. What are the Tips on writing a great review?


      • Include the "why": The best reviews include not only whether you liked or disliked a product, but also why. Feel free to talk about related products and how this item compares to them.
      • Be specific: Your review should focus on specific features of the product and your experience with it.
      • Not too short, not too long: The ideal length is 75 to 500 words.
      • Be sincere: We welcome your honest opinion about the product--positive or negative. We do not remove reviews because they are critical. We believe all helpful information can inform our customers’ buying decisions.

    3. What's not allowed?

      PC-Centre is pleased to provide this forum for you to share your opinions on products. While we appreciate your time and comments, we limit customer participation to one review per product and reserve the right to remove reviews that include any of the following: Objectionable material:
      • Obscene or distasteful content
      • Profanity or spiteful remarks
      • Promotion of illegal or immoral conduct Promotional content:
      • Advertisements, promotional material or repeated posts that make the same point excessively
      • Sentiments by or on behalf of a person or company with a financial interest in the product or a directly competing product (including reviews by publishers, manufacturers, or third-party merchants selling the product)
      • Reviews those are written for any form of compensation other than a free copy of the product. This includes reviews that are a part of a paid publicity package
      • Solicitations for helpful votes Inappropriate content:
      • Other people's material (this includes excessive quoting)
      • Phone numbers, postal mailing addresses, and URLs external to PC-Centre.com.pk
      • Comments on other reviews visible on the page (because page visibility is subject to change without notice) Store Timings
